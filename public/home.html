Weapons of Choice

In a small sampling of about 40 violent hate crime incidents documented by the New York Times, over 30% involved the assailant punching the victim, often in the head.

In about 25% of these incidents the assailant told the victim to "Go back to your country" or "Go back to China".

Around 20% of these incidents involved the assailant accusing the victim of having Covid or blaming them for bringing it to the U.S.
